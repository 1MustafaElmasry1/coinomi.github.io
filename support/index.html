Need help?

Please take some time to browse through the Frequently Asked Questions before you ask for help.
The best way to seek for help is by sending us an e-mail at: support@coinomi.com clearly describing the challenge you are facing.

Feel free to attach any screenshots or other details that you believe might be helpful (but please don't send us your seed phrase) and we'll get back to you at our earliest convenience.
